# How to Remove Digital Signatures from a PDF

> **Short answer.** To remove digital signatures from a PDF, open [Remove Signatures](/tools/remove-cert-sign), upload the signed file, and download the unsigned version. The document content remains; only the signature metadata is stripped.

A signed PDF can't be edited without invalidating the signature. If you need to make legitimate changes (fix a typo, add a page number, update metadata), you must remove the signature first, edit, then re-sign.

## What you'll need

- A signed PDF you have the right to modify.
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for handling signed documents securely.

## Step-by-step

1. **Open the tool.** Go to [Remove Signatures](/tools/remove-cert-sign).
2. **Upload.** Drop the signed PDF.
3. **Remove.** The tool strips all digital signature data.
4. **Download.** The unsigned PDF is now editable.
5. **Edit.** Make your changes.
6. **Re-sign.** Use [Certificate Sign](/tools/cert-sign) to sign the updated version.

## When removal is appropriate

- You're the original signer and need to make corrections.
- You're authorized by the signer to modify and re-sign.
- The signature is outdated (expired certificate) and the document needs refreshing.
- You need to [Flatten PDF](/tools/flatten) or [Compress PDF](/tools/compress-pdf) — both would invalidate the signature anyway.

## When removal is NOT appropriate

- You're not the signer and have no authorization.
- The signature serves as legal evidence.
- You're trying to forge or tamper with the document.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to remove signatures?

Yes. [Remove Signatures](/tools/remove-cert-sign) is free, no signup.

### Does removing a signature change the content?

No. Only the signature metadata is removed. Pages, text, and images remain identical.

### Can the removal be detected?

The document no longer has a signature. Anyone checking will see it's unsigned. There's no hidden record of the removal.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your file. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local.

### Should I remove all signatures or just one?

This tool removes all signatures. If you need to keep some, you'll need a more targeted approach with a PDF editor.

## Wrap-up

Removing digital signatures enables editing of previously locked documents. Open [Remove Signatures](/tools/remove-cert-sign), upload, download the unsigned version. After editing, re-sign with [Certificate Sign](/tools/cert-sign). Only remove signatures you have the right to remove.
