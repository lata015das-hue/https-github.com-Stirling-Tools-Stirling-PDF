# How to Rearrange Pages in a PDF

> **Short answer.** To rearrange pages in a PDF, open [Rearrange Pages](/tools/rearrange-pages), specify the new page order (e.g., 3,1,2,4), upload the file, and download the reordered version.

A scanned document came through in the wrong order. Or you want the executive summary moved before the appendix. Rearranging lets you fix the sequence without re-scanning or re-exporting from the source.

## What you'll need

- A PDF with pages in the wrong order.
- The correct order you want (write it down first).
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Open the tool.** Go to [Rearrange Pages](/tools/rearrange-pages).
2. **Enter the new order.** Type the page numbers in the order you want them: e.g., `3, 1, 2, 4` puts page 3 first.
3. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
4. **Rearrange.** Click the action button.
5. **Download.** Pages appear in your specified order.

## Rearrange vs. other operations

- **Rearrange:** change the sequence of existing pages.
- **Remove:** delete pages you don't want → [Remove Pages](/tools/remove-pages).
- **Extract:** pull specific pages into a new file → [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages).
- **Merge:** combine pages from multiple files → [Merge PDFs](/tools/merge-pdf).

## Common pitfalls

- **Wrong page numbers.** PDF page numbers and printed numbers may differ. Count from page 1 in your reader.
- **Duplicate pages in output.** If you type `1, 1, 2, 3`, page 1 appears twice. Only include each number once unless you intentionally want duplicates.
- **Large documents.** For a 200-page book, writing out all 200 numbers is impractical. Consider [Split PDF](/tools/split-pdf) into sections, reorder the sections, then [Merge PDFs](/tools/merge-pdf).

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to rearrange PDF pages?

Yes. [Rearrange Pages](/tools/rearrange-pages) is free, no signup, no watermark.

### Can I reverse the page order?

Yes. For a 5-page document, type `5, 4, 3, 2, 1`.

### Does it change the content?

No. Only the sequence changes. Each page's content remains identical.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your file. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local.

### Can I rearrange and remove pages at the same time?

Yes. Simply omit the pages you want to remove from your order. If you type `1, 3, 5` for a 5-page doc, pages 2 and 4 are effectively removed.

## Wrap-up

Rearranging PDF pages fixes ordering mistakes in seconds. Open [Rearrange Pages](/tools/rearrange-pages), specify the new order, download. For more complex operations, combine with [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages) and [Merge PDFs](/tools/merge-pdf).
