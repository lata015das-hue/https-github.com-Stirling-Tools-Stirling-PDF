# How to Scale PDF Pages to a Different Size

> **Short answer.** To scale PDF pages to a different size, open [Scale Pages](/tools/scale-pages), select the target paper size (A4, Letter, A3, Legal), upload the file, and download the resized version.

You have a document designed for Letter paper but need to print on A4. Or slides created at a custom size that need to fit standard paper. Scaling adjusts the content to fit the new page dimensions.

## What you'll need

- A PDF with pages at the wrong size.
- The target paper size you need.
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Open the tool.** Go to [Scale Pages](/tools/scale-pages).
2. **Select target size.** A4, Letter, A3, or Legal.
3. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
4. **Scale.** Click the action button.
5. **Download.** Content is resized to fit the new page dimensions.

## Common paper sizes

| Size | Dimensions | Common in |
|---|---|---|
| A4 | 210 × 297 mm | Europe, Asia, most of the world |
| Letter | 8.5 × 11 in (216 × 279 mm) | US, Canada |
| A3 | 297 × 420 mm | Large prints, posters |
| Legal | 8.5 × 14 in (216 × 356 mm) | US legal documents |

## Common pitfalls

- **Content appears smaller.** Scaling down (e.g., A3 → A4) shrinks everything. Check readability after scaling.
- **Aspect ratio mismatch.** A4 is slightly taller/narrower than Letter. Content may shift slightly.
- **Margins change.** Scaling adjusts content but may not perfectly center it. Use [Crop PDF](/tools/crop) to trim excess margins afterward.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to scale PDF pages?

Yes. [Scale Pages](/tools/scale-pages) is free, no signup.

### Does scaling change text quality?

No. PDF text is vector-based — it scales without quality loss. Only raster images may show slight differences at extreme scale changes.

### Can I scale individual pages differently?

Not directly. [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages) the pages you want to scale differently, scale each group, then [Merge PDFs](/tools/merge-pdf).

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your file. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local.

### Scale vs. Crop — what's the difference?

Scale resizes content to fit a new page size. Crop removes margins from the existing page size. They solve different problems.

## Wrap-up

Scaling PDF pages matches your content to the right paper size. Open [Scale Pages](/tools/scale-pages), pick the target, upload, download. For fine-tuning margins after scaling, use [Crop PDF](/tools/crop).
