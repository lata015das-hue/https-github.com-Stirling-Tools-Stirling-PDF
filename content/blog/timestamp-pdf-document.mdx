# How to Add a Trusted Timestamp to a PDF

> **Short answer.** To add a trusted timestamp to a PDF, open [Timestamp PDF](/tools/timestamp-pdf), upload the file, and download the timestamped version. The timestamp proves the document existed in its current form at a specific date and time, issued by a trusted authority.

A timestamp is different from a "date modified" property (which anyone can change). A trusted timestamp is cryptographic proof from a third-party Time Stamping Authority (TSA) that the document existed at that exact moment.

## What you'll need

- A PDF you want to timestamp.
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Open the tool.** Go to [Timestamp PDF](/tools/timestamp-pdf).
2. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
3. **Timestamp.** The tool contacts a TSA and embeds the cryptographic timestamp.
4. **Download.** The PDF now carries proof of existence at that point in time.

## Use cases

- **Intellectual property.** Prove you created a design, code, or document before a specific date.
- **Legal evidence.** Timestamped documents carry more weight in court than self-dated ones.
- **Regulatory compliance.** Some industries require timestamped submissions.
- **Contract disputes.** Prove a contract version existed before an alleged modification.

## Timestamp vs. Digital Signature

| Feature | Timestamp | Digital Signature |
|---|---|---|
| Proves when | Yes | No (unless combined with timestamp) |
| Proves who | No | Yes (identifies the signer) |
| Requires certificate | No (uses TSA) | Yes (your own certificate) |
| Tamper-evident | Yes | Yes |

For maximum assurance: [Certificate Sign](/tools/cert-sign) + timestamp together.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to timestamp a PDF?

Yes. [Timestamp PDF](/tools/timestamp-pdf) is free, no signup.

### Who provides the timestamp?

A Time Stamping Authority (TSA) — a trusted third party that certifies the time. The tool connects to one automatically.

### Does timestamping change the document?

The timestamp is embedded as metadata. Page content is unchanged.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your file. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local. The TSA sees a hash of your document (not the content itself).

### Can a timestamp be forged?

Not if issued by a recognized TSA. The cryptographic proof is verifiable by anyone.

## Wrap-up

A trusted timestamp proves a PDF existed at a specific moment in time. Open [Timestamp PDF](/tools/timestamp-pdf), upload, download the timestamped version. Combine with [Certificate Sign](/tools/cert-sign) for full authentication (who + when).
