# How to Remove Pages from a PDF

> **Short answer.** To remove pages from a PDF, open [Remove Pages](/tools/remove-pages), enter the page numbers to delete (e.g., 2, 4, 6), upload the file, and download the trimmed version.

A report has a blank page in the middle. A contract has an outdated appendix. A scanned batch has a page that went through sideways. Removing specific pages is faster than re-creating the document from source.

## What you'll need

- A PDF with pages you want to delete.
- The page numbers to remove.
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Identify pages.** Open in any reader, note the page numbers you want gone.
2. **Open the tool.** Go to [Remove Pages](/tools/remove-pages).
3. **Enter page numbers.** Type the pages to delete: `2, 4, 6` removes those three.
4. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
5. **Remove.** Click the action button.
6. **Download.** The output has those pages gone, everything else intact.

## Remove vs. Extract

- **Remove:** you want everything EXCEPT certain pages. Use [Remove Pages](/tools/remove-pages).
- **Extract:** you want ONLY certain pages. Use [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages).

They're inverse operations. Pick whichever describes your intent more naturally.

## Common pitfalls

- **Wrong page numbers.** Always verify in a reader first. PDF page 1 might not be printed page 1.
- **Removing the wrong page.** Keep the original as backup before removing.
- **Need to remove many pages.** If you're removing more pages than keeping, [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages) the ones you want instead.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to remove pages from a PDF?

Yes. [Remove Pages](/tools/remove-pages) is free, no signup, no watermark.

### Is the removal permanent?

In the output file, yes — those pages don't exist. Your original file is unchanged (removal creates a new file).

### Can I remove a range of pages?

Enter ranges: `5-10` removes pages 5 through 10. Mix with individuals: `2, 5-10, 15`.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your file. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local.

### Can I undo removal?

Not from the output file. Use the original to re-do the operation differently.

## Wrap-up

Removing pages from a PDF is a targeted deletion. Open [Remove Pages](/tools/remove-pages), specify what to delete, download the cleaned version. For the inverse operation, use [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages).
