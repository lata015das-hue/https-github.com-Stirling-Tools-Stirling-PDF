# How to Add a Stamp or Badge to a PDF

> **Short answer.** To add a stamp to a PDF, open [Add Stamp](/tools/add-stamp), choose your stamp type (APPROVED, DRAFT, CONFIDENTIAL, or custom text), upload the file, and download the stamped version.

Stamps communicate status at a glance: "this is approved," "this is a draft," "this is received." Adding them to a PDF after the fact — without going back to the source document — is a common workflow for legal, procurement, and quality teams.

## What you'll need

- A PDF you want to stamp.
- The stamp text or type (APPROVED, REJECTED, DRAFT, RECEIVED, or custom).
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Open the tool.** Go to [Add Stamp](/tools/add-stamp).
2. **Choose stamp type.** Select a preset or enter custom text.
3. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
4. **Stamp.** The stamp is applied to every page (or selected pages if the tool supports it).
5. **Download.** The stamped PDF is ready to share.

## Stamp vs. Watermark

- **Stamp:** a visible badge (usually colored, positioned in a corner or center). Communicates status.
- **Watermark:** diagonal semi-transparent text across the page. Communicates ownership or confidentiality.

For watermarks, use [Add Watermark](/tools/add-watermark-pdf). For stamps, use [Add Stamp](/tools/add-stamp).

## Common pitfalls

- **Stamp covers important content.** Position it in a margin or header area.
- **Need to remove the stamp later.** Stamps are burned into the page. Keep the unstamped original.
- **Multiple stamps needed.** Run the tool multiple times with different stamps, or stamp once with combined text.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to add a stamp to a PDF?

Yes. [Add Stamp](/tools/add-stamp) is free, no signup.

### Can I use a custom image as a stamp?

For image-based stamps (logos, signature images), use [Overlay PDFs](/tools/overlay-pdf) with a transparent PNG containing your stamp.

### Does it work on specific pages only?

Depends on the tool options. If not, [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages) the pages to stamp, stamp them, then [Merge PDFs](/tools/merge-pdf) back.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your file. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local.

### Can I remove a stamp after adding it?

No. Stamps are permanent once applied. Always keep the original unstamped version.

## Wrap-up

Adding a stamp to a PDF communicates document status instantly. Open [Add Stamp](/tools/add-stamp), choose your type, upload, download. For image stamps, use [Overlay PDFs](/tools/overlay-pdf). For watermarks, use [Add Watermark](/tools/add-watermark-pdf).
