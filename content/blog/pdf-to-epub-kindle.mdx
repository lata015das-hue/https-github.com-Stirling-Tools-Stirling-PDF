# How to Convert a PDF to EPUB for Kindle

> **Short answer.** To convert a PDF to EPUB for Kindle, open [PDF to EPUB](/tools/pdf-to-epub), upload the PDF, and download the EPUB. Transfer it to your Kindle via USB or email. The text reflows to fit any screen size — no more zooming and panning.

PDFs are designed for print — fixed pages, fixed fonts, fixed layout. E-readers are designed for reading — reflowable text that adapts to screen size and user preferences. Converting bridges the gap.

## What you'll need

- A text-based PDF (not a scan — if it's a scan, run [OCR PDF](/tools/ocr-pdf) first).
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Check the PDF.** Is text selectable? If yes, proceed. If no (scan), run [OCR PDF](/tools/ocr-pdf) first.
2. **Open the tool.** Go to [PDF to EPUB](/tools/pdf-to-epub).
3. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
4. **Convert.** Click the action button.
5. **Download.** Transfer the .epub to your Kindle via USB, or email it to your @kindle.com address.

## What converts well (and what doesn't)

| Converts well | Doesn't convert well |
|---|---|
| Continuous text (novels, articles) | Multi-column academic papers |
| Simple headings and paragraphs | Complex tables and charts |
| Books with linear flow | Design-heavy magazines |

The simpler the PDF layout, the better the EPUB. Complex layouts may need manual cleanup in Calibre after conversion.

## Common pitfalls

- **Scanned PDF → empty EPUB.** You skipped OCR. Run [OCR PDF](/tools/ocr-pdf) first.
- **Weird line breaks.** PDF text extraction sometimes adds line breaks mid-sentence. The EPUB reader may show these as hard returns. Manual cleanup or a tool like Calibre can fix it.
- **Images lost.** Complex image-text layouts may not transfer images correctly. For image-heavy PDFs, reading the original PDF on the Kindle app may be better than converting.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to convert PDF to EPUB?

Yes. [PDF to EPUB](/tools/pdf-to-epub) is free, no signup.

### Does it work with Kindle?

Yes. Modern Kindles read EPUB directly. Older Kindles need MOBI — use Calibre to convert EPUB → MOBI, or email the EPUB to your @kindle.com address (Amazon auto-converts).

### Will formatting be perfect?

No. PDF and EPUB have fundamentally different layout models. Expect readable text with basic formatting; don't expect pixel-perfect reproduction.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your PDF. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF keeps it local.

### What about DRM-protected PDFs?

DRM-protected PDFs cannot be converted. You need to own the content or have explicit permission.

## Wrap-up

Converting a PDF to EPUB for Kindle gives you reflowable text on any e-reader. Open [PDF to EPUB](/tools/pdf-to-epub), upload, download. For scanned content, run [OCR PDF](/tools/ocr-pdf) first. For complex layouts, expect some manual cleanup.
