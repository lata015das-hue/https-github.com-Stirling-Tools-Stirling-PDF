# How to Convert a PDF to High Quality JPG

> **Short answer.** To convert a PDF to high quality JPG, open [PDF to Image](/tools/pdf-to-jpg), select JPEG as the output format, upload the PDF, and download each page as a separate image file. For maximum quality, choose PNG instead of JPEG — it's lossless.

You need a page from a PDF as an image — for a presentation slide, a social media post, or a print shop that only accepts JPG. Converting gives you one image per page at the resolution you choose.

## What you'll need

- A PDF with the pages you want as images.
- A browser.
- Optional: self-hosted [Stirling-PDF](https://github.com/Stirling-Tools/Stirling-PDF) for privacy.

## Step-by-step

1. **Open the tool.** Go to [PDF to Image](/tools/pdf-to-jpg).
2. **Pick format.** JPEG for smaller files (lossy). PNG for maximum quality (lossless). GIF for simple graphics.
3. **Upload.** Drop the PDF.
4. **Convert.** Click the action button.
5. **Download.** One image per page. Multi-page PDFs produce multiple files.

## JPEG vs. PNG — which to choose

| Use case | Best format | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Social media / web thumbnails | JPEG | Small file, good enough quality |
| Print or archival | PNG | Lossless, no compression artifacts |
| Screenshots of text | PNG | JPEG blurs text edges |
| Photos within the PDF | JPEG | Smaller without visible loss |

## Common pitfalls

- **Blurry text in JPEG.** JPEG compresses text edges. Switch to PNG for text-heavy pages.
- **Wrong page extracted.** The tool converts ALL pages. To get just one page, [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages) first, then convert.
- **File too large for upload.** Some social platforms cap image size at 5 MB. JPEG at standard quality is usually under that; PNG often isn't. Use JPEG for uploads.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free to convert PDF to JPG?

Yes. [PDF to Image](/tools/pdf-to-jpg) is free, no signup, no watermark.

### Can I control the resolution/DPI?

The tool outputs at the PDF's native resolution. For higher DPI, some tools allow you to specify 150, 300, or 600 DPI. Check the tool's options panel.

### Does it work on scanned PDFs?

Yes. A scanned PDF is already an image — converting it to JPG just extracts that image. Quality won't exceed the original scan resolution.

### Is my file safe?

Public tools see your PDF. Self-hosted Stirling-PDF processes locally. For sensitive content, self-host.

### Can I convert back from JPG to PDF?

Yes. Use [Image to PDF](/tools/jpg-to-pdf) to combine JPGs back into a PDF.

## Wrap-up

Converting a PDF to high quality JPG is a format choice: JPEG for small, PNG for perfect. Open [PDF to Image](/tools/pdf-to-jpg), pick your format, upload, download. For single-page extraction, pair with [Extract Pages](/tools/extract-pages).
